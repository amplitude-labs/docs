## Overview

[justextract.it](https://justextract.it) is Nex AI's premier extraction suite that transforms data from any source into JSON. It powers our enterprise solutions and excels across industries e.g. manufacturing, logistics.

Our extraction surpasses traditional OCR by using a hybrid Vision Language Model (VLM) approach, ensuring the best accuracy, backed by constant testing + fine-tuning.
