## Overview

[justextract.it](https://justextract.it) is Nex AI's in-house data extraction suite that turns data from any source into JSON. It powers solutions and excels across industries such as manufacturing and logistics. Our extraction surpasses traditional OCR by using a hybrid Vision Language Model (VLM) approach, ensuring the best accuracy through constant testing and fine-tuning.
